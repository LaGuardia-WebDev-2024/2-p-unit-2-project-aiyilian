Things I want to do: Travel Edition

Camping

I'm not a super outdoorsy person per se but my mom's always really wanted to go camping so I do want to go with her one day. I also love the vibes of roasting marshmallows over a campfire with family and friends. Esccaping from the busy city life for a weekend into the woods also just sounds really comforting.



Iceland: Aurora Borealis

Iceland is a beautiful country with really amazing scenery and culture. I would love to stay at a cozy lodge or cottage in Iceland and at night me and my family would go out and look for the Aurora Borealis. I would also love to see the stars without any pollution obsturcting them. In New York it's really hard to see stars even on clear nights because of how much light pollution there is.
